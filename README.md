# DGD2003_MuhammetY-ld-z

Game Design Document: "The Lost Tour"
1. The Story
Who: You play as a student visiting a famous university. The character has a mental health condition and needs daily medicine.

The Problem: During the tour, the character realizes they forgot their pills. Because of this, they start to see delusions (things that are not real).

The Goal: You must find a way to the University Clinic to get help while escaping the monsters in your mind.

2. Main Mechanics
A. The "Mind Bar"
This bar shows how much the character is losing touch with reality.

High Bar: Everything looks normal. The university is quiet and bright.

Low Bar: The walls start to shake. You hear strange voices. The monsters appear and can hurt you.

B. The Monsters (The Delusions)
The monsters are symbols of the character's fears:

The Faceless Proctor: A tall monster that follows you slowly. If it catches you, it forces you to "take an exam," which drains your energy.

The Whispers: Small shadows that hide in corners. They make the screen blurry and make it hard to move.

C. Fight or Run
Running/Hiding: You can hide under library tables or inside lockers. This is the safest way to play.

Fighting: You can use "Mental Energy" to push monsters away or use objects like heavy books to hit them.

The Catch: If you fight too much, the "Mind Bar" drops faster. You become more "lost" in the delusion.

3. The Changing World
The university changes based on your mental state:

In the Normal State, a hallway might be a dead end.

In the Delusion State, that same hallway might grow and become a long bridge. You must use these changes to find new rooms.

4. Winning the Game
To win, you must reach the Medical Center. At the end, the character finds their medicine and the monsters disappear. The game ends with the character sitting safely on a bench, realizing the campus is just a normal school again.
